react-polar-gg

A visualisation library that implements a grammar of graphics. Written in ReactJS + Ecmascript 6.

It was originally written to plot monthly data onto a variety of polar plot but got expanded to support a variety of plots onto a variety of coordinates.

Modular architecture. New graph elements are easy to add.
